4-H Values in Action 🌟

💚 Head - Thinking

We use critical thinking and problem-solving skills to design, build, and improve our rockets through scientific methods.

❤️ Heart - Caring

We support each other, mentor new members, and share our passion for rocketry with the community.

🙏 Hands - Doing

We learn by building, testing, and flying rockets while developing practical engineering and leadership skills.

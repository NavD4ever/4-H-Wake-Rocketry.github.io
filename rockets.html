Youth-Led Design Process üé®

Every rocket is designed and built by our youth members with adult mentorship. We start with OpenRocket simulations, then move to hands-on construction using balsa, fiberglass, and 3D-printed components. Safety is our top priority, with every rocket inspected before flight. Through building rockets, our members learn engineering principles, problem-solving, and teamwork skills that last a lifetime.
